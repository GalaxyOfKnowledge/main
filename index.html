TRIVIA LASER
Answering trivia questions correctly charges your "Trivia Laser".
Press "T" to fire a powerful laser that destroys all enemies on screen.
Wrong answers drain your laser charge.
TIME BONUSES
Fast Answers: Answer quickly for temporary invincibility or slow-mo.
Slow/Wrong Answers: Enemies become faster and shoot more often.
WAVE SYSTEM
The game progresses through waves of enemies.
After each wave, you'll face a trivia question.
Correct answers make the next wave easier, wrong answers make it harder.
At wave 15, you'll face the final boss battle!
